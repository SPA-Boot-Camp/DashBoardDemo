DISCRIPTION ABOUT BIM(BACHELOR OF INFORMATION MANAGEMENT)

Bachelor of Information Management is four years program of Tribhuvan University that provides knowledge of Management Information and Communication System. This program helps to develop software skills in object oriented software design methods and data Management systems. The theory and practical teaching and learning with case studies, guest lectures, research work, assignments, project work and term papers are covered by BIM. TU, BIM provides indepth understanding of trends of software to manage the information of an Organization.

The Bachelor of Information Management (BIM) is a special purpose program in semester system designed to develop socially responsible, scientifically approached / creative, and result oriented Information Technology (IT) professionals. The program intends to equip students with the skills and attributes required to be effective and efficient IT professionals.

The course structure is divided into Foundation courses 24 credit hours, business courses 30 credit hours, IT and Computer courses 60 credit hours, Elective courses 6 credit hours, Internship and projects of six credit hours. Total course is 126 credit hours. The syllabus integrates Business Management, Programming, System Administration, Operating system, Electronic reporting and Auditing, Mathematics and Liberal Arts into the curriculum.
